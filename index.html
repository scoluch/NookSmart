How it works

Five simple steps from battery pack to smart lighting.

  1. Remove the battery pack from your book nook kit
  2. Install the NookSmart module
  3. Connect it to your WiFi network
  4. Add it to Apple Home or Alexa
  5. Control your lights with voice, phone, or schedule

Setup typically takes under 5 minutes.

FAQ

Does this require a special hub?

NookSmart connects directly to WiFi. For Apple Home automation, you may need a Home hub (HomePod mini, Apple TV, or iPad configured as a hub). For Alexa voice control, you’ll need an Echo or compatible Alexa device.

Does it work with Apple HomeKit?

Yes. It works with Apple Home, Siri, and Amazon Alexa.

Can I dim the lights?

Yes—brightness can be set anywhere from 1% to 100%.

Can I install multiple NookSmart devices?

Yes. Each book nook can be controlled independently.
