FAQ

Does NookSmart work with Rolife book nook kits?

Yes. Most Rolife kits use a battery box with 2 AAA batteries and a pair of wires twisted to the LED light wires. NookSmart is compatible with these—twist your LED wires to the optional wire adapter and plug it into NookSmart. Select the wire adapter at checkout if your kit uses twisted wires rather than plastic connectors.

Does NookSmart work with CuteBee book nook kits?

Yes. NookSmart is compatible with typical CuteBee miniature LED connectors. If you are unsure about your specific kit, send a message on Etsy and we will help confirm fit.

Does NookSmart work with MiniCity book nook kits?

Yes. NookSmart is compatible with typical MiniCity miniature LED connectors. If you are unsure about your specific kit, send a message on Etsy and we will help confirm fit.

What is a battery eliminator for book nooks?

A battery eliminator replaces your kit’s battery pack with permanent wired power—usually USB—so you never swap batteries again. NookSmart is a smart battery eliminator: it plugs into USB power and adds WiFi, voice control, scheduling, and dimming for Rolife, CuteBee, MiniCity, and DIY book nook kits.

Does this require a special hub?

NookSmart connects directly to WiFi. For Siri automation, you may need a Home hub (HomePod mini, Apple TV, or iPad configured as a hub). For Alexa voice control, you’ll need an Echo or compatible Alexa device.

Does it work with Siri?

Yes. It works with Siri and Amazon Alexa.

Can I dim the lights?

Yes—brightness can be set anywhere from 1% to 100%.

Can I install multiple NookSmart devices?

Yes. Each book nook can be controlled independently.

Does the book nook come with it?

No—NookSmart is a lighting controller for book nook and diorama kits you already have. Your miniature house, LEDs, and touch switch (if included) are separate.

Does NookSmart work with dollhouse or diorama lighting?

NookSmart is designed for book nook kits and similar miniature house and diorama projects that use a small battery pack and 3–5V miniature LEDs. It is not a general dollhouse wiring system—if your setup uses the same type of battery box and LED connectors as popular book nook kits, it may fit. Send a photo of your connectors on Etsy and we'll confirm.
